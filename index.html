Tips for physical labelling

  • ✦Write codes lightly on the back of printed photos in pencil to avoid ink bleed-through.
  • ✦For mementos (tickets, postcards), use a small adhesive label on the back — never write directly on originals.
  • ✦Paragraphs reference multiple photos: list all codes in the text, e.g. "(→ 2019-PH-012, 2019-MT-003)".
  • ✦The sequence resets per year & type, so 2019-PH-001 and 2020-PH-001 are distinct.
  • ✦Use the Export Photos (ZIP) to download all uploaded files with their new coded names already applied.
